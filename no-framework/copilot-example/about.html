About SUNY New Paltz

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1828

Our History

Founded in 1828, SUNY New Paltz has evolved from a normal school to a prestigious public university, serving generations of students in the Hudson Valley region.

Mission Statement

We are committed to providing high-quality, affordable education to students from all backgrounds, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and creativity.

Rankings & Recognition

Consistently ranked among the best regional universities in the Northeast by U.S. News & World Report.
