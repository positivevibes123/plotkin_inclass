Admissions

Welcome to the SUNY New Paltz admissions page! Here you will find all the information you need to apply.

Admissions Process

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application.
  2. Submit your high school transcripts.
  3. Provide letters of recommendation.
  4. Write a personal statement.
  5. Check your application status online.

Requirements

To be considered for admission, you must meet the following requirements:

  • A high school diploma or equivalent.
  • A minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT) are optional.

Important Dates

Make sure to keep track of these important dates:

  • Application Deadline: January 15
  • Decision Notification: March 1
  • Enrollment Deposit Due: May 1
